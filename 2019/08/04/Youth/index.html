Youth
发布于：2019-08-04 08:58:10 访问：

源于百词斩的一篇阅读分享

Youth is not a time of life; it is a state of mind;

it is not a matter of rosy cheeks, red lips, and supple knees;

it is a matter of the will, a quality of the imagination, a vigor of the emotions;

it is the freshness of the deep springs of life.

Youth mean a temperamental predominace of courage over timidiity, of the appetite for adventure over the love of ease.

This often exsits in a man of sixty more than a boy of twenty.

Nobody grows old merely by a number of years,

We grow old by deserting our ideas.

Years may wrinkle the skin, but to give up the enthusiasm wrinkles the soul.

Worry, fear, self-distrust bows the heart and turns the spirit back to dust.

Whether sixty or sixteen, there is in every human being’s heart the lure of wonders, the unfailing child-lke appetite of what’s next, and the joy of game of living.

In the center of your heart and my heart, there is a wrieless station;

so long as it receieves the messages of beauty, hope, courage and power from man and from the Infinite, so long as you are young,

When the aerials are down, and your spirit is covered with snows of cynicism and ice of pessimism, then you’ve grown old, even at twenty. but as long as your aerials are up, to catch the waves of optimism, there is hope you may die young at eighty.
